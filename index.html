ABOUT ME

I'm Ankit Suman, a software engineering graduate student at Ontario Tech University (MEng, 2026–2027) based in Oshawa, Canada. My background is in backend development — Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs — but game development is where I'm directing my creative energy.

I started building games out of curiosity and fell in love with the craft. I work primarily in Godot 4 with GDScript, building everything from 3D platformers to 2D shooters and top-down RPGs. I care about tight controls, satisfying mechanics, and clean code.

My software engineering background gives me a strong foundation in problem-solving, debugging, and writing maintainable systems — skills that translate directly into game development. I'm actively building my portfolio and looking for opportunities to grow in the industry.
